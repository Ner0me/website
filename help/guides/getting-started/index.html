# Getting started

# Installation

Paperback is currently not on TestFlight and requires the usage of AltStore in order to install onto your phone. In the future, TestFlight will be used when the lead developer obtains an Apple Developer Account.

Video tutorial

If you prefer using a video tutorial, Artesians made a beautiful one. You will find it here for Windows.

# Prerequisites

  1. On your phone, download the app through the front-page of this website. The app will download as Paperback.ipa.

    Alternatively you can download through our GitHub releases page.

  2. Download AltServer for your operating system.

Pre-releases

If you're interested in the features introduced in the Pre-release or Stable builds then you need to be a Patron to use the app.

Warning

This requires iOS 13.3+ or iPadOS 13.4+, the app will CRASH otherwise. Paperback is NOT designed for any iOS below.

# AltStore instructions for Mac

  1. Move "AltServer.app" to your Applications folder and launch it.
  2. Connect your iPhone or iPad to your computer and make sure it's unlocked and your device is trusted.
  3. Mojave users need to open iTunes and enable "iTunes Wi-Fi sync" for your phone. Catalina users need to open Finder and enable "Show this iPhone when on WiFi" for your phone.
  4. Click the AltServer icon in the Mac menu bar and click "Install AltStore" and select your device.
  5. Enter your Apple ID email and password. (BOTH email address and password are case sensitive).

    Your Apple ID and password are sent to Apple, and nowhere else.

    • AltServer will ask you to install a Mail plug-in, follow the instructions to continue. This is only required the first time.
  6. AltStore should be installed to your phone.

# AltStore Mail plug-in for Mac

  1. Click the "Install Mail Plug-in" from the AltServer menu.
  2. Enter your password to grant AltServer permission to install the plug-in.
  3. Restart the Mail app and open preferences (CMD+,).
  4. Click "Manage Plug-ins" and enable "AltPlugin.mailbundle".
  5. Finally, click "Apply and Restart Mail" to finish installation.

# AltStore instructions for Windows

  1. Download and install iTunes for Windows. This will not work if you have the Windows Store app version of iTunes.
  2. Download and install iCloud for Windows 8. This will not work if you have the Windows Store app version of iCloud.

    Can't find the link to download iCloud for Windows 8? Image

  3. Restart your PC after installing both iTunes and iCloud. This will not work if you did not restart your computer.
  4. Extract the downloaded AltInstaller.zip file. Run setup.exe and follow the instructions.
  5. Launch AltServer from your Notification Area/System Tray or Hidden Icons.

    Can't find AltServer? Image

  6. Connect your iPhone or iPad to your computer and make sure it's unlocked and your device is trusted.
  7. Open iTunes and enable "iTunes Wi-Fi sync" for your phone.
  8. Click the AltServer icon and click "Install AltStore, then choose your device.
  9. Enter your Apple ID email and password. (BOTH email address and password are case sensitive).

    Your Apple ID and password are sent to Apple, and nowhere else.

  10. AltStore should then be installed into your phone.

# Paperback Installation

  1. On Mac: Open Mail and AltServer. On Windows: Open AltServer along with iCloud and iTunes.
  2. Make sure you're on the same connection (same router, in most cases) as the computer running AltServer. If you're not connected to the same network, you can connect your device via cable.
  3. Trust AltStore application: on your device go in Settings, General, Profiles & Device Management. Choose your Apple ID and select Trust.
  4. Launch AltStore on your device.
  5. Go to My Apps and tap the + icon in the top left corner.
  6. Open the .IPA you downloaded from the prerequisites section.
  7. Paperback should finally be installed!

# Installation FAQ

# I get "Access Denied" when trying to install AltStore

Run AltServer as an administrator, and ensure that you've restarted after installing iTunes and iCloud.

# I can't find the .IPA file to download

Navigate down toward the assets drop down, the .IPA should be listed there. Image

# I have some other issue that isn't listed here

Please, check altstore.io/faq/. If you still can't resolve the issue visit the #support channel in our Discord for fast reponse or submit a post on Reddit.

# App Troubleshooting

If you're having problems with the application, please check the App Troubleshooting page. If you're having an issue that is not in the troubleshooting page, please use the #support channel on Discord to ask questions.
